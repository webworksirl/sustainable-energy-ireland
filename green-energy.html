Inverter: An inverter plays a critical role by converting the DC power generated by the panels into alternating current (AC), the type of power most Irish households use.

Optimiser: Optimisers enhance the output performance of the PV system by optimizing the voltage of each individual PV panel (module) continuously to maximize power output.

The Battery: As your solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, any excess energy is stored in the battery, which your home can access when panel output is low.

EV Charger: For quick and efficient electric vehicle (EV) charging, an EV charger facilitates maximum charging rates. It seamlessly works with your solar PV system, directing excess electricity to your EV instead of the grid.

Diverter: The diverter monitors power export and diverts excess energy to your immersion for heating water. This feature ensures a higher self-consumption rate from your system.
