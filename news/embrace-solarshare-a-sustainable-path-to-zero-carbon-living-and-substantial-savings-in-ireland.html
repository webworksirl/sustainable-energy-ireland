Embrace SolarShare: A Sustainable Path to Zero-Carbon Living and Substantial Savings in Ireland"

Posted by SolarShare on 2023-10-27

 

The environmental impact of traditional energy consumption is staggering. A typical detached home in Ireland, equipped with two cars, emits a staggering 15 tons of carbon annually. However, by embracing SolarShare's technology, homeowners can make an immediate and substantial difference. Solar PV panels harness the sun's energy, allowing households to generate clean electricity right at home. This reduces the dependence on Ireland's grid, which still relies heavily on fossil fuels.

The financial savings are equally impressive. By connecting to SolarShare, homeowners can potentially save up to €800 per year on their electricity bills, and by participating in the SolarShare Virtual Power Plant, they could save nearly €25,000 over the life of their system. These savings are a testament to the power of renewable energy and the long-term benefits it offers.

Solar panels installed by SolarShare enable homeowners to generate up to 80% of their electricity needs. This increased self-reliance reduces the reliance on conventional grid electricity, which is often carbon-intensive.

SolarShare's commitment to sustainability is not only about reducing carbon emissions but also providing an affordable and efficient way for homeowners to adopt renewable energy. By installing solar PV panels and energy storage batteries, households can reduce their annual expenses by up to €800 and start a journey towards a zero-carbon lifestyle.

Furthermore, the benefits of solar energy extend far beyond individual homes. Homeowners who make the switch contribute to the local economy by reducing the need to import fossil fuels. This ensures energy security, reducing the risk of supply disruptions due to international conflicts or environmental catastrophes abroad. Additionally, switching to solar power results in an annual reduction of approximately 1 metric ton of carbon emissions per average home. Over the system's lifetime, this equates to preventing over 25 metric tons of greenhouse gas emissions, contributing to a cleaner environment for future generations.

SolarShare stands out as a reliable and experienced partner for solar PV installation in Ireland. With a history of successful installations and satisfied customers, SolarShare offers tailored solutions that cater to the unique needs of each homeowner. They take care of the Building Energy Rating (BER) certificate and provide excellent after-sales service, ensuring homeowners have support whenever they need it.

Real customers have also shared their positive experiences with SolarShare. Customers have praised the professionalism, knowledge, and support provided throughout the installation process. They appreciate SolarShare's efficiency, professional installation, and the system's exceptional performance.

For those considering the cost of solar panels in Ireland, the price typically ranges from €6,000 to €17,000, before applying SEAI grants. Eligible homeowners can save up to €2,400 with these grants. Additionally, the Clean Export Guarantee scheme allows homeowners to be compensated for any excess electricity their panels generate and don't use. The government's decision to exempt VAT on solar panel purchase and installation further adds to the potential savings, making solar panels a cost-effective choice.

The advantages of solar panels in Ireland are clear. With the potential to generate up to 80% of electricity needs, reduce reliance on the grid, and make a positive environmental impact, SolarShare is at the forefront of this sustainable energy revolution. It's a win-win situation that benefits homeowners' wallets and the planet. SolarShare's dedication to personalized, cost-effective, and efficient solar solutions, along with their support in grant applications, makes them a top choice for harnessing the benefits of solar energy in Ireland. Contact SolarShare today to begin your journey to a sustainable and cost-effective energy future.

In conclusion, SolarShare's solar PV panels and energy storage batteries offer a transformative way for Irish homeowners to reduce their carbon footprint, save on energy bills, and contribute to a cleaner environment. By generating clean electricity from the sun, homeowners can embark on a path to zero-carbon living while enjoying significant financial benefits. SolarShare's commitment to tailored solutions, experience, and excellent after-sales service make them the ideal partner for those looking to embrace sustainable energy solutions in Ireland.
