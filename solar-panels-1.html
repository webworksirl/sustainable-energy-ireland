Battery for Electricity Storage

In addition to solar panels, you may choose to install a battery. Solar Power Financing This is crucial if you aim to utilize all the electricity generated in your home. Solar power should either be used immediately or stored, as excess energy is exported to the grid. The battery provides storage for surplus energy until you need it.

You also have the option to sell any excess energy back to the grid, with different providers offering varying rates per kWh, known as the Feed-In Tariff (FIT). Some homeowners forego batteries to reduce costs and opt to sell excess power back to the grid. An in-depth review of your solar survey data will help determine whether you need a battery.

    3 Ways Solar Panels Benefit Everyone

    By adopting solar energy, you contribute to more than just personal savings. Homeowners who install solar panels provide substantial benefits to society as a whole:

    Economic Boost: By generating their own power, homeowners reduce the need to import fossil fuels, bolstering the local economy and decreasing reliance on foreign energy sources.

    Energy Security: Lower dependence on foreign fuels ensures consistent access to affordable energy, reducing the risk of supply disruptions due to international conflicts or environmental catastrophes abroad.

    Reduced Carbon Footprint: Switching to solar power results in an annual reduction of approximately 1 metric ton of carbon emissions per average home.
