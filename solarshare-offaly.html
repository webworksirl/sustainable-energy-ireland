    Will I get paid for electricity generated and sent to the grid?

    Yes, you will be compensated for excess power generated by your panels. In February 2022, legislation was passed in The Dáil to enable the payment of a Feed-In Tariff for surplus electricity sent back to the grid. By the end of August 2022, most companies will declare their rates for this excess power. Some announced rates include Pinergy at 13.5 cents per kWh, SSE Airtricity and Electric Ireland at 14 cents per kWh, Energia at 18 cents per kWh, and Bord Gais at 18.5 cents per kWh.
